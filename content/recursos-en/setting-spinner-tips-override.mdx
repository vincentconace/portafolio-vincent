## What it is

`spinnerTipsOverride` lets you replace the text that rotates on screen while Claude is thinking. `excludeDefault: true` removes the defaults; your `tips` array replaces them.

Use cases: team-specific humor, reminders, brand voice.

## Summary table

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| aitmpl category | `global` |
| Installs | 36 |
| Setting key | `spinnerTipsOverride` |

## How to install it

```bash
npx claude-code-templates@latest --setting global/spinner-tips-override
```

Edit the `tips` array with your own messages.

For Claude:

```
I installed the global/spinner-tips-override setting. Show me the default
array and replace it with [my team's custom tips].
```

## What you can do with it

### Consistent brand voice

Your org has a specific tone. You replace the defaults with your own phrases.

### Team reminders

"2 days left until sprint review" as a spinner tip.

### Team humor

Funny tips that only your team gets.

### Combo with [Friday Deploy Warning](/boveda/setting-friday-deploy-warning)

Friday Deploy is a specific case. Spinner Tips is more generic.

## When it's NOT worth it

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **Only 36 installs:** very niche.
- **If the defaults are enough for you:** the override is overhead.
- **For individual use:** less useful than team-shared.

</Callout>
