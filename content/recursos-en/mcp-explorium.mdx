## What it is

Explorium is a B2B data platform: it catalogs 150M+ businesses, 800M+ professionals and 4,000+ signals (fundings, hiring, tech stack, etc.) compiled from 50+ sources. It competes directly with ZoomInfo and Apollo in the B2B data space.

This MCP plugs the Explorium API into Claude Code. You ask Claude "build me a list of Heads of Engineering at B2B SaaS companies with 100-500 employees in Latam that grew headcount 50%+ in the last year" and Claude queries Explorium, filters by signals and returns the list.

Useful for sales, lead generation, market research and quick due diligence.

## How to install it

```bash
npx claude-code-templates@latest --mcp web-data/explorium
```

Prerequisite: Explorium account + API key.

For Claude:

```
I installed the web-data/explorium MCP. Once the credentials are set,
build me a list of 50 B2B SaaS companies in the United States with
50-200 employees that raised a Series A in the last 12 months.
Give me back the name, domain, headcount, funding date and CEO.
```

## What you can do with this

### Segmented lead lists

"Build me a list of companies in `<industry>` with `<criteria>` and filter by `<signal>`". The MCP returns CRM-ready records.

### Enrichment of existing leads

"I have this list of 200 emails. Enrich it with current title, company, headcount, tech stack". Useful for SDRs.

### Hunt companies in the moment

"Companies that have had `<signal>` (fund raising, expansion, executive hire, layoffs) in the last 30 days, in `<sector>`". Useful for outreach timing.

### Competitive mapping

"Companies that show up as competitors of `<company>` in public signals". To understand the landscape.

### Tech stack intelligence

"Of the 100 largest SaaS companies in Europe, what % use `<your competitor product>`?". Useful for battlecards.

## Prerequisites

- **Active Explorium account** (enterprise plans)
- **API key**
- **Use cases that fit B2B** (not for B2C or private individuals)

## When it's NOT worth it

<Callout type="warning" title="Before you use it">

- **Without a paid account, it doesn't work:** there's no significant free tier.
- **Data quality varies by geography:** for very small markets, coverage may be lower.
- **Compliance:** GDPR / CCPA apply. Coverage of private professionals is a gray area depending on jurisdiction. Validate.
- **vs [Apollo](/boveda/plugin-claude-plugins-official) / ZoomInfo:** they're competitors. If you already have one working, don't add another unless it has different coverage.
- **Only 1 install:** not much community testing yet. Try it in a sandbox.

</Callout>
