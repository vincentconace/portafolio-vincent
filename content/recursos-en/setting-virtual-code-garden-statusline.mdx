## What it is

A gamified, garden-themed statusline. Your code is a garden:
- **Plants evolve** from seeds to trees based on activity
- **Dynamic weather effects** (sun, rain, etc.)
- Calmer than [Tamagotchi](/boveda/setting-programmer-tamagotchi-statusline) (you don't need to "feed" anything)

For users who prefer calmer, aesthetic gamification over combat/competitive.

## How to install it

```bash
npx claude-code-templates@latest --setting statusline/virtual-code-garden-statusline
```

For Claude:

```
I installed the statusline/virtual-code-garden-statusline setting. Show me
the current state of my garden and the weather.
```

## What you can do with this

### Calmer gamification

If Tamagotchi or RPG is too much, garden is chill.

### Aesthetic nature

For users who enjoy the slow-growth metaphor.

### For focus sessions

The garden doesn't demand attention; it's just growing. It's not disruptive.

## When it's NOT a good fit

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **For serious work:** [Context Monitor](/boveda/setting-context-monitor) is the option.
- **If the nature theme doesn't resonate:** it's a gimmicky alternative.

</Callout>
