## What is Scrapling

Picture the internet as a giant supermarket and you want a list of every yogurt with its name, price, and brand. **Scrapling is your robot** that walks aisle by aisle, notes down each yogurt, and builds the list in Excel for you. **The stores never realize** it was a robot.

Technically: an open source Python library that extracts info from any website (Google Maps, directories, social media, stores) **without getting blocked**. It disguises itself as a real human browser.

## Advanced tricks

### Stealth mode

Scrapling uses **Camoufox** (an anti-detection browser). Ask Claude to enable it for sites with strict Cloudflare.

### Proxy rotation

For large lists (1000+ records), configure rotating proxies. Claude can integrate them with a proxy pool MCP.

### Auto-match selectors

Scrapling detects changes in a site's HTML and **updates the selectors on its own**. Your scrapers don't break even when the site changes.

## Ethical scraping — your business depends on it

### Respect robots.txt + ToS

If a site says "no scraping", **don't scrape it**. Some violations can lead to lawsuits.

### Rate limiting

Scrapling already waits between requests. **Do NOT speed it up**. Burning a site burns you.

### Don't touch sensitive personal data

❌ Private addresses, medical data, private info
✅ Commercial contacts from Google Maps (public, the business itself published them so you'd contact it)

### GDPR / LFPDPPP

If you contact the leads:
- Identify yourself
- Explain where you got their data
- Offer an opt-out

**A transparent email sells 10x better than spam**.

---

## Anti-patterns

### 1. Speeding up the rate limiting

Wanting 10x speed → you get banned → you lose access. **Patience**.

### 2. Heavy LinkedIn scraping with Scrapling

LinkedIn detects and blocks easily. For LinkedIn → [Apify](/boveda/apify-clientes-ilimitados).

### 3. Selling a list without contacting them

A list without effective outreach = a waste of time. **Sell the outreach too**, not just the list.

### 4. No disclaimer when reaching out

If you contact someone without saying where you got the data, **it's pure spam**. Disclaimer + transparency.

### 5. Skipping the MCP setup

The MCP is what makes the flow magic. Without the MCP, you can use Scrapling as a regular library but you lose the integration with Claude.
