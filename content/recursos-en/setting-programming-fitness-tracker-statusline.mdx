## What it is

A statusline that reimagines coding as exercise. It shows:
- **Steps** (arbitrary mapping of activity)
- **Calories burned** (also arbitrary)
- **Badges** based on milestones
- **Activity intensity** levels

It's clearly gimmicky — you're not burning real calories by coding. For users who enjoy the health metaphor.

## What you can do with this

### Health metaphor

If seeing "calories burned" motivates you, even if it's fictional, it can boost engagement.

### Gamification with badges

Earning badges = small dopamine hits.

### For casual presentations

Visually different, it grabs attention.

## When it's NOT a good fit

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **Only 24 installs:** the least adopted of the gimmicky cluster.
- **For serious work:** [Context Monitor](/boveda/setting-context-monitor).
- **If you like real fitness tracking:** this setting gets confused with real apps (Apple Health, etc.).
- **Problematic health metaphor:** a lot of people confuse sedentary coding with exercise. It can be counterproductive.

</Callout>
