## What it is

Another gimmicky statusline from the catalog's "fun" family. Casino theme: the statusline shows chips you win or lose based on random dice rolls while you code. Your fortune rises and falls throughout the session.

It belongs to the decorative line alongside [Bug Circus](/boveda/setting-bug-circus-statusline), [Code Spaceship](/boveda/setting-code-spaceship-statusline), [Data Ocean](/boveda/setting-data-ocean-statusline).

## What you can do with it

### Gamification

If games motivate you, the wins/losses tracking can add something to routine sessions.

### For long sessions

The casino theme can help keep engagement up during marathon debugging.

### Decorative

If your setup is the "customize everything" style, it pairs well with other gimmicky touches.

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">
