## What it is

Firecrawl is a web scraping platform designed specifically for LLM apps: instead of handing you raw HTML (which the model then has to parse), it gives you clean, structured markdown. It handles SPAs, JavaScript-heavy sites, and basic anti-bot.

For Claude Code, this means: give it a URL, and the MCP returns the content in a format the model processes efficiently. No tokens wasted on repeated `<div class="...">`.

Compared to [Apify](/boveda/mcp-apify) (an Actor catalog) or [Bright Data](/boveda/mcp-brightdata) (enterprise scale), Firecrawl is simpler and more focused: "give me this URL as LLM-ready markdown."

## What you can do with this

### Turn a URL into context

"Scrape `<URL of X library's docs>` for me and use that content as context to answer my next question." Anti-hallucination when Context7 doesn't cover the library.

### Crawl an entire site

"Crawl the site `<docs.mydomain.com>` and bring me all the pages as a single unified markdown." Handy for indexing your own documentation.

### Structured extraction

"From this product page, extract `name`, `price`, `description`, `images` as JSON." Firecrawl supports schema-based extraction.

### Recurring research

"Every Monday, scrape pages X, Y, Z (competitor changelogs) and bring me a diff against last week." Combine it with a scheduler.

### Reading long articles

"Scrape this article and summarize the key ideas in 5 bullet points." Cleaner than copy-pasting the HTML.

## Prerequisites

- **Firecrawl account** with an API key
- **A reachable, public URL** (it won't scrape things behind your login)
- **Comply with the scraped site's TOS:** robots.txt, ToS

## When it's NOT a good fit

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **For industrial-scale scraping:** [Bright Data](/boveda/mcp-brightdata) has more infrastructure.
- **For sites with your own login:** Firecrawl doesn't authenticate with your session. For that, use [Browser Harness](/boveda/browser-harness) or [browser-use](/boveda/mcp-browser-use-mcp-server).
- **For sites with serious anti-bot:** Firecrawl handles the basics (light Cloudflare), but LinkedIn / Instagram require dedicated tools.
- **Costs:** the free tier has a cap. For high-volume use, check the pricing.
- **Compliance / legal:** scraping sites with strict TOS is your responsibility.

</Callout>
