## What it is

**Absolute #2 among statuslines (2,551 installs).** Only behind [Context Monitor](/boveda/setting-context-monitor) (10k+).

A statusline with ANSI color codes to tell info apart by color:
- **Blue** — model
- **Green** — directory
- ...and more

No gimmick, no theme. Just useful color. That's why it has more adoption than the themed ones.

## What you can do with it

### Fast visual scan

Instead of reading text, the colors take you straight to the info that matters.

### Compatible with modern terminals

ANSI colors work in macOS Terminal, iTerm, Ghostty, Linux terminals, and modern Windows Terminal.

### Professional without being gimmicky

For teams where gimmicky statuslines are inappropriate, this is the serious option with visual appeal.

### Combo with [Context Monitor](/boveda/setting-context-monitor)

Context Monitor is info-rich with progress bars. Colorful is lightweight with color. Compare them by preference — don't install both (there's only one statusline).

## When it's NOT a good fit

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **Terminal without ANSI support:** colors show up as weird escape codes.
- **If you want more info:** [Context Monitor](/boveda/setting-context-monitor) has progress bars and analytics.
- **For low-contrast accessibility terminals:** check readability.

</Callout>
