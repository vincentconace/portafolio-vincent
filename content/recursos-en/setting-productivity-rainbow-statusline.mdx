## What it is

A celebratory statusline. It shows:
- **Rainbow colors** that cycle over time
- **Energy levels** based on the time of day
- **Productivity streaks** you accumulate

Somewhere between [Colorful Statusline](/boveda/setting-colorful-statusline) (useful color) and the gimmicky themed ones. A mix of productivity tracking + visual fun.

## How to install it

```bash
npx claude-code-templates@latest --setting statusline/productivity-rainbow-statusline
```

For Claude:

```
I installed the statusline/productivity-rainbow-statusline setting. Show me
my current energy levels and my streak.
```

## What you can do with it

### Gentle gamification

Track streaks without being intrusive.

### Color and energy

Rotating color + energy level gives you something "alive" in the statusline.

### For users who enjoy visual feedback

If bright colors motivate you, this delivers.

## When it's NOT a good fit

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **If it distracts you:** rotating colors can get annoying.
- **For serious work:** [Context Monitor](/boveda/setting-context-monitor) is the option.
- **If your terminal doesn't support colors:** the codes show up as weird text.

</Callout>
