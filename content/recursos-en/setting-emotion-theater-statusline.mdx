## What it is

Another statusline from the gimmicky family. Theme: "emotional theater":
- **Mood faces** rotating at random
- **Activity detection** based on the file types present in the project

For example, if your project has `.py` files, it shows "researching"; if `.js`, "scripting"; if `.md`, "writing".

```bash
npx claude-code-templates@latest --setting statusline/emotion-theater-statusline
```

## What you can do with this

### Personality in the editor

If you like a bit of humor in your workflow, this adds it.

### Activity detection

Unlike other gimmicky ones, this one provides real info: it tells you what kind of work is happening based on your files.

### For casual presentations

In team demos, the mood faces can break the ice.

## When it's NOT worth it

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **For serious work:** [Context Monitor](/boveda/setting-context-monitor) is the grown-up option.
- **If random moods distract you:** avoid it.
- **For formal presentations:** not very professional.

</Callout>
