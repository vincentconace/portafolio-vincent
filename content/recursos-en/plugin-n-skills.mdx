## What it is

N-Skills is a small, curated marketplace by Numman Ali. Just 5 plugins, but every one of them passed his personal filter. The philosophy is the opposite of the giant 100+ marketplaces: less is more, each plugin has a clear purpose and earns its slot.

It works cross-tool: Claude Code, Codex and openskills. If you rotate between tools, N-Skills follows you.

The 5 plugins:
- `zai-cli` — Z.AI: vision, search, reader and GitHub exploration via MCP
- `gastown` — multi-agent orchestrator with its own vocabulary (convoys, polecats, rigs, slinging work)
- `dev-browser` — browser automation with persistent page state (by Sawyer Hood)
- `orchestration` — multi-agent coordination for tasks that break down in parallel
- `open-source-maintainer` — end-to-end maintenance of open-source repos

## What you can do with this

### Browser automation with persistent state

`dev-browser` keeps state between calls. You log in once, run several actions — the browser doesn't restart between each one. The key difference from [Playwright Skill](/boveda/plugin-playwright-skill), which is model-invoked with no maintained state.

### Maintain an open-source repo without losing your mind

`open-source-maintainer` covers the four typical pain points: triaging new issues, reviewing incoming PRs, analyzing contributor activity, generating repo health reports. If you maintain an OSS project solo, it earns its slot.

### Serious multi-agent orchestration

`orchestration` is designed specifically for tasks that break down into parallel subtasks. When a single Claude session isn't enough because there are 8 things to do at the same time, this plugin coordinates them.

### Its own vocabulary with Gastown

`gastown` is interesting: the author built an orchestrator with its own words ("convoys", "polecats", "rigs", "slinging work"). The usage is: you speak in that vocabulary and Claude understands what to orchestrate. It sounds gimmicky but it helps when you want to trigger specific behaviors without writing long prompts.

### Z.AI as additional tooling

`zai-cli` gives you access to Z.AI (vision, search, reader) via MCP. Useful if you want capabilities that the Claude/Codex stack doesn't cover.

## When it's NOT a good fit

<Callout type="warning" title="Before you install">

- **If you want a marketplace that covers everything:** N-Skills is 5 focused plugins. For variety, check out [buildwithclaude](/boveda/plugin-buildwithclaude) or [Claude Plugins Official](/boveda/plugin-claude-plugins-official).
- **`gastown` requires learning its own vocabulary:** if you don't want to invest in that, it's just an ornament.
- **`zai-cli` needs a Z.AI account:** without it, the plugin is useless.
- **For browser automation without maintained state:** [Playwright Skill](/boveda/plugin-playwright-skill) may be simpler.

</Callout>
