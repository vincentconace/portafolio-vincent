## What it is

The equivalent of [Discord Detailed](/boveda/hook-discord-detailed-notifications) and [Slack Detailed](/boveda/hook-slack-detailed-notifications) for Telegram. It sends messages with detailed info:
- Working directory
- Duration
- System info

Hook events: `SessionStart` + `Stop`.

Prerequisite: a Telegram bot + a chat ID to send to (you create the bot via @BotFather).

## How to install it

```bash
npx claude-code-templates@latest --hook automation/telegram-detailed-notifications
```

Prerequisites:
- A bot created with @BotFather (you receive a token)
- A chat ID (channel or personal)

For Claude:

```
I installed the automation/telegram-detailed-notifications hook. Configure
TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN and TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID. Run a session and verify
that it arrives.
```

## What you can do with this

### Notifications on your phone

Telegram has the best mobile experience of the three platforms. Great for tracking on the go.

### Teams in countries where Telegram dominates

LATAM, Eastern Europe, parts of Asia. If your team is there, Telegram > Slack.

### Personal channels

A private bot with just you = your own tracking channel without team noise.

### Combo with [Telegram Error Notifications](/boveda/hook-telegram-error-notifications)

Detailed for success + Error for problems = complete visibility.

## When it's NOT worth it

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **If you don't use Telegram:** go with Slack or Discord.
- **Bot setup overhead:** creating a bot, getting a chat ID, configuring it. If it's casual use, it's not worth it.
- **Compliance:** Telegram has policies. For enterprise teams, check first.
- **Spam:** short sessions will flood your chat.

</Callout>
