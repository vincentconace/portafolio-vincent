## What it is

This is the official marketplace that Anthropic maintains specifically for [Claude Cowork](/boveda/dominar-cowork) — not the programming-focused Claude Code, but the office "coworker." If your world is Gmail + Calendar + Notion + Slack + Drive and you want Claude to move fluidly across those tools, this is **the** marketplace.

39 plugins in total, organized by role/function. The philosophy is to verticalize: instead of "Claude that does everything," it gives you a role-specific pack (sales, finance, legal, marketing, etc.) that already ships with the workflows and templates for that job.

11k stars indicate that the community adopted it massively. And since it's from Anthropic, it comes with the official guarantee.

## How to install it

```bash
# 1) Marketplace
/plugin marketplace add anthropics/knowledge-work-plugins

# 2) Install the packs for your role
/plugin install productivity@anthropics-knowledge-work-plugins
/plugin install enterprise-search@anthropics-knowledge-work-plugins
/plugin install sales@anthropics-knowledge-work-plugins  # or marketing, finance, etc.
```

Or `/plugin` → Discover.

For Claude (in Cowork):

```
Install the anthropics/knowledge-work-plugins marketplace. My role is
[sales / marketing / finance / etc.] and my stack is [Gmail + Notion +
Slack + HubSpot, for example]. Recommend the 3-4 packs to start with.
```

## What you can do with this

### Have a productive Cowork from day 1

The `productivity` pack sets up tasks + calendar + context memory. The `enterprise-search` pack lets you search everything at once. With just those two, you go from "Claude answers questions" to "Claude works with you in the office."

### Cover an entire role without hiring a specialist

If your team doesn't have a PM, install `product-management` and Claude assists you with specs, roadmaps, user research synthesis. If you don't have legal, `legal` helps you with NDAs and contract review. It doesn't replace the specialist, but it fills a lot of gaps.

### Consistent brand voice automatically

`brand-voice` (from Tribe AI) discovers your voice from existing docs, generates guidelines, and validates AI-generated content. Useful for marketing teams tired of manually reviewing every output.

### PDFs with real annotations

`pdf-viewer` lets you view and annotate PDFs inside Cowork. Mark up contracts, signatures, forms. Without having to open another app.

### Integrate with your stack without writing MCPs

Salesforce Slack, Apollo, ZoomInfo, Mintlify, Zapier — all verified by Anthropic. You connect without wrestling with setup or worrying about security.

## When it's NOT worth it

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **If you use Claude Code (not Cowork):** the packs are designed for Cowork. Much of what they offer doesn't apply to a dev in the terminal.
- **If you're not on the Claude Max plan:** [Cowork requires the Max plan](/boveda/dominar-cowork). Check first.
- **Don't install all 39 packs at once:** you'll have 118 skills loaded. Pick 3-4 based on your role.
- **Overlap with [Claude Small Business](/boveda/claude-small-business):** Small Business also covers roles. See which fits you before installing both.
- **Some integrations require paid accounts** (Apollo, ZoomInfo, Mintlify). The plugin connects — you pay for the account.

</Callout>
