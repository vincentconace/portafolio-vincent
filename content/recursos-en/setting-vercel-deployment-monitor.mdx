## What it is

Statusline focused on Vercel deployments. It shows:
- **Build status** in real-time
- **Time** since the last deploy
- **OSC 8 hyperlink** that's clickable and opens the deployment

OSC 8 is the escape sequence for hyperlinks in modern terminals — clicking on the statusline opens the URL.

## What you can do with it

### Build status awareness

Without opening the Vercel dashboard, you can see whether the deploy went through.

### Open the deploy in one click

Clicking the link takes you to the deployment in the browser.

### For fast iteration

Edit → push → see the build in the statusline → click to verify live.

### Combo with [Vercel Auto Deploy Hook](/boveda/hook-vercel-auto-deploy)

The hook triggers the deploy. The statusline shows the status. A natural combo.

### Combo with [Vercel Multi Env Status](/boveda/setting-vercel-multi-env-status)

Single Deploy Monitor vs. Multi Env. You can only have one statusline — choose based on your needs.

## When it's NOT worth it

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **No Vercel:** doesn't apply.
- **If your terminal doesn't support OSC 8:** the click won't work, but the text still does.
- **Production deploys only:** [Multi Env Status](/boveda/setting-vercel-multi-env-status) covers staging/preview as well.

</Callout>
