## What it is

The minimal version of the Telegram pipeline. When Claude or a subagent finishes, it sends a message without detail.

Hook events: `Stop` + `SubagentStop`.

Prerequisites:
- `TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN` (via @BotFather)
- `TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID`

## How to install it

```bash
npx claude-code-templates@latest --hook automation/telegram-notifications
```

Configure it:

```bash
export TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN="..."
export TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID="..."
```

## What you can do with this

### Alert to your phone

Telegram has the best mobile UX. Notification to your phone when it finishes.

### Personal channel

A private bot with yourself = a tracking channel without a team.

### Subagent completions

If your workflow launches subagents, each one notifies you.

## When it's NOT worth it

<Callout type="warning" title="Before installing">

- **Without Telegram:** use the Slack/Discord variants.
- **Without bot setup:** it won't work.
- **For frequent short sessions:** overload.

</Callout>
