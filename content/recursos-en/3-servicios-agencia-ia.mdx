## Sales strategy — the order

> **DON'T sell everything in one shot.**

Start with a small, concrete service. The client tries it. When they see results, they raise the ticket. Then you scale up to the full ecosystem.

### Typical path

```
Month 1-2: Marketing (site + SEO audit) → $500-2000
Month 3-4: Add Leads (WhatsApp + lead gen) → $2000-5000
Month 5+: Full ecosystem → $5000-15000+
```

Raising the ticket on proven value vs. selling everything at once and not delivering.

## Anti-patterns

### 1. Selling the ecosystem from day 1

New client + ecosystem = **you burn out**. You have to deliver 10 things at once with no track record.

**Start with service 1, earn trust, scale**.

### 2. Charging by the hour

Charge by **outcome** or a **fixed retainer**. By the hour you cap your upside and the client gets anxious watching the clock tick.

### 3. Not documenting the system

If you're the only one who understands how the client's setup is built, **you become a slave to it**. Document so your team can maintain it.

### 4. Accepting EVERY client

Some never buy the next level. Say no to clients who can't upgrade. **Focus on the ticket**.

### 5. Unbranded skills

If you just "install generic skills", **anyone can copy you**. Your value is **the complete system + your knowledge of the client**.
